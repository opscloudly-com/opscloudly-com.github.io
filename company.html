Our mission

At OpsCloudly, we are dedicated to transforming the way revenue organizations source and close deals. Our mission is to provide our customers with an unfair advantage over both their competitors and potential customers through insight and analysis. We’ll stop at nothing to get you the data you need to close a deal.

We’re customer-obsessed — putting the time in to build a detailed financial picture of every one of our customers so that we know more about your business than you do. We are in this together, mostly because we are all implicated in large-scale financial crime. In our history as a company, we’ve never lost a customer, because if any one of us talks, we all go down.

Meet the team

Founded by an all-star team.

OpsCloudly is founded by two of the best sellers in the business and backed by investors who look the other way.

Years ago, while working as sales associates at rival companies, Thomas, Ben, and Natalie were discussing a big client they had all been competing for. Joking about seeing the terms of each other’s offers, they had an idea: what if they shared data to win deals and split the commission behind their companies’ backs? It turned out to be an incredible success, and that idea became the kernel for OpsCloudly.

Today, OpsCloudly transforms revenue organizations by harnessing illegally acquired customer and competitor data, using it to provide extraordinary leverage. More than 30,000 companies rely on OpsCloudly to undercut their competitors and extort their customers, all through a single integrated platform.
